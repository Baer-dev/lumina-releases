# Lumina — release notes

## v1.0.1 — July 19, 2026

- **The refresh button shows her working.** Rewriting the brief now spins
  the ↻ and dims the old words while she writes the new ones. And if a
  rewrite fails, the earlier brief stays put instead of vanishing.
- **She's honest about missing news.** If your feeds can't be reached,
  the brief says so plainly, in her own words. She won't guess at
  headlines that didn't arrive. I tested it for exactly that.
- **A dead connection can't hang the brief.** Feed fetches now give up
  after a few seconds instead of waiting forever.
- **She can say "Ollama" — and her own name.** (American voices could
  not pronounce "Lumina" until now. She could only say it in a British
  accent, which I admit is a bit funny.)
- **Updating over a running Lumina just works.** The installer now closes
  her quietly first — no locked-file complaints, no stuck dialogs.

That's all for now.

# Lumina — releases

**A little AI who lives on your computer.** She chats, speaks, listens,
remembers — and everything runs on your own Windows machine.

This repo is the shelf:

- **[INSTALL.md](INSTALL.md)** — the buyer's walkthrough, including the
  click-path through the two scary-looking Windows warnings
- **`version.json`** — what Lumina's optional update check reads (one GET,
  anonymous, off by default)

**The source lives at [baer-dev/lumina](https://github.com/baer-dev/lumina)**
— v1.0.0 exactly as it shipped, source-available under PolyForm Noncommercial.

**The installer lives on [Gumroad](https://baerdev.gumroad.com/l/lumina)** —
$15, once: the assembled app, updates through v1.x, and a developer who
answers his own email.
